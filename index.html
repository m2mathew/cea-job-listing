DevOps Engineer

As our first-ever DevOps Engineer, we'll look to you to help define and implement our long term infrastructure and development strategy. For 11 years we've been able to make do by learning on the fly, but we recognize that it's time to bring in a dedicated and experienced specialist.

Our engineers are very experienced, so you'll have a good team to help shape your ideas. But, we need you and your infrastructure experience to help determine what we're doing well and where we need to improve. This role is definitely not for someone looking to maintain the status quo, nor do we need a big-company specialist in one particular technology. We're looking for someone that brings experience, abilities, and ideas across a wide range of infrastructure components.

A few of the things you'll do:

  • Improve/manage our existing cloud infrastructure (AWS, Azure, Heroku)
  • Develop and implement a plan for migrating any remaining infrastructure components to the cloud
  • Implement a Continuous Delivery Solution that supports our current multi-app environment (Git, SVN, .NET, IIS, Node.js, Javascript, React.js) and includes automated testing
  • Ensure high availability and resiliency across our systems
  • Server management, maintenance and security for Windows and Linux
  • Design and implement a secure but functional networking solution for our cloud environment
  • Establish system monitors and alerts
  • Leverage containerization (Docker, etc …) to provide dynamic resource allocation
  • Support our development team and help us build and deliver high-quality software quickly and efficiently

We are counting on your contributions to immediately boost the development speed and reliability of the products and services we provide. As our infrastructure specialist, you will help set the priorities to maximize your impact. You will wear many hats and work across a variety of different technologies. At times you will pull from your past experiences to find an elegant solution, other situations may require you to learn quickly and “just make it work”. Either way, you'll be joining a passionate team of individuals that like to have fun and build great software. If this sounds like your cup of tea, this might be the place for you.

Ready for some challenges and good fun? We'd love to hear from you.
