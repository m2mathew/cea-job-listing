.NET/C# Software Engineer

You'll be an integral part of a small engineering team with many years of experience. We'll expect you to learn quickly and make critical and significant contributions. Currently, we're working on the next version of our web application, increasing our calling capacity, moving more of our infrastructure to the cloud, and improving our automated testing suite.

You'll be a key player in developing and maintaining the web services that support our internal and external applications. Most of these APIs are written in C# communicating with a SQL Server database. In addition, we are constantly discussing new ideas and evaluating other technologies that can improve our product.

Requirements:

  • Passionate self-starter that can learn quickly
  • Strong C# foundation
  • Experience building robust APIs
  • Working knowledge of Microsoft SQL Server
  • Show us some code that you've written

Bonus points if you have:

  • Worked with Jenkins or other CI Tool
  • Deployed applications using AWS or other PaaS provider
  • Experience with other languages or frameworks (eg. Javascript, Node.js)

Ready for some challenges and good fun? We'd love to hear from you.

Full Stack Web Developer

Call-Em-All is looking for a software engineer to help build the next version of our web application and beyond. We're not concerned with what stack you're an expert in, we just want awesome engineers. We'd rather see some code that you're proud of than a resume that says how you've mastered "all the things."

You'll work with our development team to build new functionality, and our customer service team to resolve any problems as they occur. We love new ideas and embrace healthy conflict. We will NOT force you to attend countless soul-draining status meetings, or report to three different managers. We will empower creativity and stay out of the way so you can make us better.

Requirements:

  • Passionate self-starter that can learn quickly
  • Value good design and really care about user experience
  • Languages: Javascript, HTML, CSS
  • Libraries/Frameworks: Node.js, React
  • Show us some code that you've written

Bonus points if you have:

  • Familiarity with flux architecture (we are using Redux)
  • Working knowledge of Jenkins or other CI Tool
  • Experience deploying apps to AWS or Heroku

Ready for some challenges and good fun? We'd love to hear from you.

Customer Experience Intern

We are a small, tight-knit unit so chances are you'll get to wear a variety of hats. Our interns are not only given meaningful work throughout their tenure, but they're also given a chance to walk in the shoes of professionals who greet every challenge head-on and celebrate every victory as a team. View our Manifesto (https://www.call-em-all.com/manifesto) to learn a little more about who we are.

We make it a point to challenge our interns and provide valuable experience that will grow you professionally and directly impact the direction and success of the Call-Em-All platform. You'll be a trusted team member who will work with us side by side (not stuck in a cube next to the Keurig).

You'll acquire a real-world education from a multitude of avenues. You'll have the opportunity to work directly with our customers through email, chat, and phone communication to ensure they're finding success with Call-Em-All. Additionally, you'll gain an understanding of how our Sales and Account Development teams function with Customer Support in both daily and long-term operations. Our goal is to make a real, positive impact with our users and you'll be right in the middle of it all. We hope you're ready!

If this sounds interesting, we'd love to hear from you!
